Our Technical Services

What We Fix & Handle

From brand-new pool startups to complex equipment failures, we have the training and tools to get your system running right.

🚀

Pool Startups

Opening a new pool or restarting after winter? A proper startup ensures your equipment is primed and water is balanced from day one — preventing costly mistakes down the road.

  • Initial water fill & chemical startup
  • Equipment priming & testing
  • Filter media inspection & prep
  • Pump & motor startup check
  • Salt cell activation (if applicable)
  • Full system run & verify
  • Owner walk-through & tips
🔍

Equipment Troubleshooting & Repair

Strange noises, low flow, error codes, or the pump just stopped? We diagnose accurately before recommending any repair — honest assessment, transparent pricing.

  • Pump & motor diagnostics
  • Heater error code diagnosis
  • Filter integrity testing
  • Salt chlorinator cell testing
  • Leak detection (equipment pad)
  • Valve & plumbing checks
  • Impeller & seal replacement
  • Part sourcing & installation
🤖

Automation Control

Pool automation systems let you control everything — lights, pumps, heater, spa jets — from your phone or wall panel. We install, program, and troubleshoot leading brands.

  • Pentair EasyTouch / IntelliCenter
  • Hayward OmniLogic setup
  • Jandy iAquaLink programming
  • Wireless remote configuration
  • Smartphone app integration
  • Variable speed pump scheduling
  • LED light control setup
💬

Consultations

Not sure what your pool needs? Buying a home with a pool? Thinking about an upgrade? We provide expert, honest advice — no sales pressure, no jargon.

  • Pre-purchase pool inspections
  • Equipment upgrade recommendations
  • Energy efficiency assessments
  • Water chemistry education
  • DIY guidance for owners
  • Pool renovation planning
🏆

PHTA Certified Technicians

We hold Pool & Hot Tub Alliance (PHTA) certification — the gold standard for pool professionals. That means every diagnosis and repair follows industry best practices, protecting your investment and your family.
