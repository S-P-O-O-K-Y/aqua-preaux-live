Our Cleaning Services

We offer three maintenance tiers to fit every pool size, budget, and schedule.

📅

Weekly Full Service

Our most popular plan. We come out every week to keep your pool in perfect condition — you just enjoy it.

  • Water chemistry testing & balancing
  • Brushing walls, steps & waterline
  • Skimming surface debris
  • Vacuuming pool floor
  • Emptying skimmer & pump baskets
  • Backwashing filter as needed
  • Equipment visual inspection
  • Written service report every visit
🏠

Residential Cleaning

Tailored care for homeowners who want a beautiful pool without lifting a finger. Flexible scheduling to fit your life.

  • Full chemical analysis & treatment
  • Algae prevention treatments
  • Tile & waterline brushing
  • Debris removal & vacuuming
  • Filter inspection & cleaning
  • Pump & heater check
  • Smartphone-friendly service updates
  • Same technician every visit
🏢

Commercial Cleaning

Hotels, gyms, HOAs, and apartment complexes rely on us to keep shared pools safe, compliant, and guest-ready.

  • Health code compliance checks
  • High-frequency service schedules
  • Detailed log & service records
  • Chemical bulk management
  • Multi-pool property coordination
  • Emergency response availability
  • Customized service agreements
  • Priority scheduling
Why It Matters

The Benefits of Regular Service

💧

Crystal Clear Water

Balanced chemistry means no green water, cloudy haze, or algae buildup.

🛠️

Longer Equipment Life

Proper maintenance prevents pump, filter, and heater damage from imbalanced water.

🏊

Safer Swimming

Correct sanitizer levels protect swimmers from harmful bacteria and viruses.

💰

Saves You Money

Preventative maintenance is far cheaper than emergency repairs or full pool restoration.
